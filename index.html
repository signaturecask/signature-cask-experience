The Spirit

Inchgower 16 Year Old Single Malt
A coastal Speyside treasure. Complex and robust, this expression delivers notes of sea salt, toasted hazelnuts, and a lingering citrus spice. Aged to perfection in oak, it is a testament to the distiller's art.

The Ritual of Smoke

To unlock the full character of the Signature Cask, place the smoker atop your glass. Ignite the artisanal smoking chips until a thick, aromatic cloud fills the vessel. Let the smoke dance with the spirit for 30 seconds before lifting the lid. This infusion of charred wood adds a deep, primeval layer to the Inchgower’s coastal profile.

Nose Salted caramel and dark chocolate.
Palate Spicy orange peel and nutty malt.
Finish Long, warming, with a hint of briny smoke.
Cask Ex-Bourbon and Sherry finish.
