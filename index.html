THE PROVENANCE

Distilled in the coastal town of Buckie, this 16-year-old expression has matured in a single first-fill Bourbon Hogshead (Cask 812264B). The high 55.9% Vol preserves the natural oils and maritime salinity unique to the Inchgower distillery.

THE SMOKING CHIPS

These artisanal chips were harvested from retired French Oak staves. They have been lightly toasted to release vanillins and tannins that specifically counteract the high-proof heat of this malt, creating a velvet-smooth marriage of smoke and salt.
