Signature Infusions

1. The Neat Reunion
Smoke the cloche for 30s using 3 cask chips. Pour 50ml Inchgower 16. Let the smoke settle for 10s. The purest form of the "Closed Loop."
2. The Founder’s Old Fashioned
50ml Inchgower 16, 10ml honey syrup, 2 dashes walnut bitters. Stir with ice, strain into a smoked glass. The wood smoke elevates the honey notes.
3. The Cask-Charred Sour
Shake 50ml whisky, 25ml lemon, 15ml sugar, and egg white. Double strain into a glass that has been cold-smoked for 45s. A creamy, smoky revelation.
